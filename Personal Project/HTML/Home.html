Training

With data we allow swimmers to access customized training plans that cater to their specific requirements. Whether it's for endurance, speed, stroke technique, or strength training, we can create structured programs that help them progressively improve.

Progress

Swimmers need to monitor their performance over time to identify strengths and weaknesses. We track various metrics such as lap times, stroke count, heart rate, splits, and more. This data helps swimmers see their improvement, set goals, and stay motivated.

Improvement

Swimming involves complex techniques across various strokes (freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly). We offer video tutorials, drills, and tips to help swimmers improve their form, which is crucial for both performance and injury prevention.
